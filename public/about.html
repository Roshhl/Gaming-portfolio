About Me

Hi i'm Rochelle, a self taught frontend developer and a Law and Business graduate, with a growing passion for tech, design, and creative problem solving. In early 2025, I made the decision to seriously transition into the tech space, driven by the desire to find a career that not only challenges me intellectually but also allows me to express my creativity. I began teaching myself the basics, starting with HTML and CSS, then quickly moved on to JavaScript, spending hours learning foundational concepts through various online resources. After building my first project, a to do list app, I realised I had found a field that truly excites me. From there, I explored different roles and discovered that frontend development and UX/UI design were the perfect fit for my problem solving mindset and love for storytelling through design. I specialise in building responsive, user focused websites that work seamlessly across devices. Whether it is frontend development, UX/UI layout, or creative web design, I am passionate about bringing bold ideas to life.

Why work with me?

I bring a unique creative twist to every project. I care deeply about capturing a client's vision and turning it into something visually striking and fully functional. Whether you are looking for a clean website or something immersive and gamified, I will ensure your digital experience is as bold and user friendly as it is effective. I believe no idea is too ambitious. If I can imagine it, I will find a way to build it.
